Introduction

Welcome to the official documentation for ExophsBot, a Minecraft bot designed for the 5B5T server.

This bot provides utility, fun, movement, and communication commands to enhance your gameplay experience.

All public commands are prefixed with ! and can be used in in-game chat or via DiscordSRV bridge.

Note: This page lists public commands only. Admin commands (starting with :) are not shown here.

For questions or suggestions, join our Discord using !discord.

Command Usage Description

Utility & Info

!help !help Shows a link to this documentation page.
!ping !ping [username] Shows your ping or another player's ping (returns "unknown" if not available).
!info !info Shows bot runtime info (Node.js version, OS, CPU model).
!prefix !prefix Displays the current command prefix (currently "!").
!uptime !uptime Shows how long the bot has been running (in minutes).
!online !online Shows the current number of players online on the server.
!discord !discord Shows the invite link to the official Discord server.

Message History

!lastmsg !lastmsg Shows the most recent message sent by the specified player.
!firstmsg !firstmsg Shows the first recorded message sent by the specified player.
!randommsg !randommsg Picks and displays a random message from the player's history.

Fun / Troll Commands

!say !say Makes the bot say your message (blocks real commands, § codes, newlines, very long messages)
!gay !gay [target] Shows a random funny percentage of how "gay" you or another player are (joke).
!iq !iq [target] Shows a random IQ score (40–160) for you or another player (joke).
!pp / !dick / !cock !pp [target] Shows a random "pp size" in cm (6–24 cm) for you or another player (joke).
!autistic !autistic [target] Shows a random percentage of how "autistic" you or another player are (joke).
!retard / !retardness !retard [target] Shows a random percentage of "retardness" (joke command).
!fat !fat [target] Shows a random percentage of how "fat" you or another player are (joke).
!silly !silly [target] Shows a random percentage of how "silly" you or another player are (joke).
!roast !roast Sends a random savage/funny roast directed at the specified player.
!8ball !8ball Ask the Magic 8-Ball a question and get a random classic response.
!y/n !y/n Answers any question with a random YES or NO.
!doxx !doxx [target] Shows a fake random IP address (purely for jokes — not real doxxing).
!coords !coords Shows completely fake random coordinates (meme/troll command).

Offline Messaging

!offlinemsg !offlinemsg Sends an offline message — the player sees it when they join (or sometimes when they chat).
!ignore !ignore Blocks a player from sending you offline messages via !offlinemsg.
!unignore !unignore Removes a player from your ignore list for offline messages.

Movement & Combat

!move !move [time or look params] Makes the bot walk, jump, stop, or look in a direction (supports timed movement and yaw/pitch).
!goto !goto Makes the bot pathfind to the specified coordinates.
!come !come Makes the bot walk toward the player who used the command.
!follow !follow Makes the bot continuously follow the specified player.
!attack !attack Makes the bot attack a player or certain animals with crits. Use "stop" to cancel.

Other / Misc

!kit !kit Fake kit claim message (currently cosmetic only).
!rules !rules Shows basic server rules and how to report players.
!report !report [reason] Creates a fake/public report message for the specified player.
!health !health Shows the bot's current health and hunger values.
!hunger !hunger Shows the bot's current hunger and health values.
!pitch !pitch Shows the bot's current camera pitch value.
!yaw !yaw Shows the bot's current camera yaw value.
